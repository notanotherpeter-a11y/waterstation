[ 04 ] Our Standards

We Take Water
Quality Seriously.

  • 🧪
    Monthly Lab Testing
    Our water is tested every month by an accredited laboratory. Results are posted at the station — no secrets, full transparency.
  • 🔆
    UV Sterilization
    UV light kills 99.9% of bacteria and viruses — without chemicals. Your water is biologically safe, naturally.

    Every jug is washed, rinsed, and UV-sanitized before refilling. Never worry about container cross-contamination again.

[ 08 ] FAQ

Common Questions.

Do I need to bring my own jug? ▼
Yes, for refilling you need to bring your own container. However, we also sell brand-new jugs if you need one. We sanitize every container before filling, regardless of where it came from.
What is the difference between purified, mineral, and alkaline water? ▼
Purified water has contaminants removed via RO and UV. Mineral water has essential minerals re-added after purification. Alkaline water has a higher pH (7.5–9.5), which some prefer for better hydration and antioxidant properties.
Is same-day delivery really available? ▼
Yes! Orders placed before 2:00 PM are delivered the same day. Orders placed after 2PM will be scheduled for the next morning. We'll confirm via WhatsApp once your order is received.
How do I know the water is safe? ▼
We are DOH-licensed and our water undergoes monthly laboratory testing. Results are displayed at our station and available upon request. Our 7-stage purification process ensures zero bacteria and contaminants.
Do you offer subscriptions or regular delivery schedules? ▼
Yes! You can set a weekly or bi-weekly delivery schedule. Just let us know your preferred day and quantity — we'll automatically add you to our delivery route and you won't have to re-order every time.
What is the minimum order for free delivery? ▼
Orders totaling ₱200 or more get free delivery. Below ₱200, there's a flat ₱20 delivery fee. Bulk orders (office/events) always include free delivery regardless of amount.
